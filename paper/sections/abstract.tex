\begin{abstract}
We present \textbf{ConjLean}, an end-to-end system for automated mathematical
conjecture generation, symbolic pre-filtering, Lean~4 formalization, and
four-layer proof search, paired with \textbf{REFUTE}, a multi-agent
counterexample-search pipeline.
REFUTE coordinates three specialized agents: an R-Agent that applies four
complementary search strategies (boundary sweep, random structured sampling,
symbolic perturbation, and analogical transfer), an S-Agent that selects
strategies using a UCB1 bandit policy over cross-conjecture success statistics,
and a C-Agent that proposes refined conjectures when counterexamples are found.
We evaluate on a 92-conjecture benchmark spanning three difficulty tiers and
three mathematical domains.
Using Qwen2.5-Math-7B-Instruct, REFUTE achieves precision $1.00$,
recall $0.292$ ($95\%$ CI $[0.19, 0.39]$), and
F$_1$ $0.452$ ($95\%$ CI $[0.32, 0.57]$) with zero false positives across
all 21 refutations.
